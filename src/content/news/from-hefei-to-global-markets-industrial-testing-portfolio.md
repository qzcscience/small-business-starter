WAYEAL's public product catalog places industrial testing instruments alongside online monitoring instruments, laboratory analytical instruments, medical instruments, and chromatography consumables. Within industrial testing, the portfolio includes helium leak detectors, pressure decay leak detectors, and vacuum chamber leak detection systems.

The industrial testing portfolio is built for manufacturers that need to verify sealing performance in demanding applications. Public product pages list solutions for new energy, semiconductor, automotive, refrigeration, home appliance, power, and conventional vacuum industry use cases. These markets often require more than a standalone detector; they need a test method that matches the part, cycle time, leak rate target, and production environment.

For mobile and modular helium leak detection, WAYEAL presents multiple SFJ-series instruments for vacuum and sniffer testing workflows. For chamber-based testing, the company lists vacuum chamber leak detection systems for applications such as semiconductor components, lithium batteries, automotive radiators, home appliances, and power equipment.

This range helps production teams choose between localized inspection, integrated line testing, and chamber-based testing. It also gives engineering teams a path to scale from validation work to automated production quality control.

As WAYEAL continues to serve international markets, the company positions its industrial testing portfolio around practical leak detection methods, system integration, and application-specific equipment selection.
