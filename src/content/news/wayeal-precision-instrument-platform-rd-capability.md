WAYEAL, also known as Anhui Wanyi Science and Technology Co., Ltd., was established in 2003 and listed on the Shanghai Stock Exchange Science and Technology Innovation Board in 2020. Public company information describes WAYEAL as a precision scientific instrument manufacturer with business coverage across industrial testing instruments, online monitoring instruments, laboratory analytical instruments, and medical and life sciences instruments.

That broad platform is important for customers who need stable measurement, repeatable quality control, and long-term service support. Leak detection is not an isolated product category for WAYEAL. It sits alongside chromatography, mass spectrometry, environmental monitoring, and other instrument technologies that all depend on sensing accuracy, data control, and reliable system engineering.

WAYEAL's public company profile also highlights continued investment in research and development, including postdoctoral research workstations, academician workstations, and national enterprise technology center capabilities. These platforms support the development of instruments that must perform in production environments as well as in laboratory and validation settings.

For industrial customers, this matters most at the point where testing equipment becomes part of a repeatable manufacturing process. Whether the application is helium mass spectrometry, vacuum chamber leak detection, pressure decay testing, or environmental monitoring, the instrument must provide consistent results while fitting into the customer's operating workflow.
