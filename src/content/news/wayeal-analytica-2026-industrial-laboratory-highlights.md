analytica 2026 took place from March 24 to March 27, 2026, at Messe München. The official exhibitor profile listed Anhui Wanyi Science and Technology Co., Ltd. - WAYEAL as a publicly listed industrial and laboratory analytical instruments manufacturer from China.

The profile introduced WAYEAL's four product lines: analytical instruments, industrial leak detector and system products, environmental monitoring systems, and medical devices. For industrial testing visitors, the listed product scope included helium mass spectrometer leak detectors, vacuum chamber helium leak detection systems, and pressure decay leak testers and systems.

This combination made analytica 2026 a useful setting for conversations across laboratory analysis and production quality control. Many customers who work with chromatography, environmental monitoring, or process validation also need dependable leak testing when sealed components, vacuum equipment, refrigerant circuits, batteries, or precision assemblies are involved.

For the leak testing side of the portfolio, discussion topics included method selection, integration needs, and how to choose between mobile helium leak detectors, modular detectors, and chamber-based systems. These are practical questions for manufacturers that need to connect measurement performance with production workflow.

WAYEAL's participation at analytica 2026 reflected its wider international positioning as a precision scientific instrument manufacturer serving universities, institutes, industrial companies, and global partners.
