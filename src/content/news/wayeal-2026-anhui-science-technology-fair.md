From April 26 to 28, 2026, the Fourth China (Anhui) Science and Technology Innovation Achievement Transformation Fair was held at Hefei Binhu International Convention and Exhibition Center. Organized by the Anhui Provincial People's Government, the event focused on translating innovation into practical industrial value and strengthening the role of enterprises in advanced manufacturing.

WAYEAL joined the high-end equipment manufacturing exhibition area with three core scientific instruments: the IC6300 intelligent ion chromatograph, the LCMS-TQ9200 liquid chromatography-tandem mass spectrometry system, and the SFJ-16D mobile helium mass spectrometer leak detector. Together, the products showed how WAYEAL connects laboratory analysis, precision measurement, and industrial leak detection capability in one instrument platform.

## High Visitor Interest In Advanced Instrumentation

During the fair, the WAYEAL booth drew visitors from industry, universities, research organizations, and enterprise teams. Product specialists introduced instrument performance, application scenarios, and workflow details, creating active discussions around high-end scientific instruments and their role in manufacturing quality control.

For many visitors, the booth provided a direct look at domestic precision instrument development across both analytical testing and industrial inspection. The conversations also reflected growing market interest in reliable measurement platforms that can support production, research, environmental monitoring, food safety, biomedicine, and new energy applications.

## Three Products Highlight Core Technology

The IC6300 series intelligent ion chromatograph is designed as an integrated ion chromatography platform. It supports multiple detection modes, including direct conductivity, suppressed conductivity, amperometric detection, and conductivity-amperometric series detection. It can also generate hydroxide, carbonate, or methanesulfonic acid eluent systems online, making it suitable for environmental monitoring, disease control, food safety, pharmaceutical, and chemical applications.

The LCMS-TQ9200 liquid chromatography-tandem mass spectrometry system combines WAYEAL's LC3600 ultra-high-performance liquid chromatograph with the TQ9200 triple quadrupole mass spectrometer. Built around core technologies such as an ultra-high-pressure linear motor pump, a high-stability electrospray ion source, and efficient ion transmission optics, the platform is designed to deliver dependable quantitative analysis for complex samples.

The SFJ-16D mobile helium mass spectrometer leak detector uses a cart-based ergonomic structure for flexible production and laboratory deployment. It supports vacuum helium spray testing, sniffer testing, helium hood testing, and back-pressure testing, with a minimum detectable leak rate of 5.0 x 10^-13 Pa.m3/s. The instrument is positioned for high-sensitivity leak detection in fields such as new energy and semiconductor manufacturing.
