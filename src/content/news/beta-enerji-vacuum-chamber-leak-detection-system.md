Wayeal recently delivered a vacuum chamber leak detection system for Beta Enerji's new flagship factory in Turkey, adding another customer-success milestone for automated leak testing in the energy equipment sector.

The project reflects a simple but important reality in modern production: when the factory is built for scale, quality control has to move with it. For transformers and related power equipment, sealing integrity is not a side check. It is part of the product's reliability, performance, and service life.

![Wayeal vacuum chamber leak detection system prepared for Beta Enerji's production site](/images/news/beta-enerji-vacuum-chamber-leak-detection-system.jpg)

## A Production Step Built for Repeatability

The delivered system is designed to support a repeatable vacuum chamber workflow. Parts can be loaded, evacuated, tested, and released with a clear pass-fail result, giving the production team a controlled inspection step that is easier to standardize than manual judgment.

That matters in high-volume manufacturing because leak detection is not only about finding defects. It is also about keeping the line moving with less variation, fewer rechecks, and better traceability from one batch to the next.

![Vacuum chamber leak detection equipment for automated production quality control](/images/news/beta-enerji-vacuum-chamber-machine-photo-2.jpg)

## Why High-Sensitivity Leak Testing Matters

In energy equipment manufacturing, even a small leak path can create larger problems later. A weak seal can reduce confidence in the assembly, trigger rework, or create field reliability risks after the product leaves the factory.

Automated vacuum chamber testing helps manufacturers catch those issues early. By turning sealing quality into a measurable checkpoint, the process supports consistent output and protects downstream performance where it matters most.

![Wayeal vacuum chamber leak detection machine details for Beta Enerji delivery](/images/news/beta-enerji-vacuum-chamber-machine-photo-3.jpg)

## Supporting Beta Enerji's Growth

For Wayeal, customer success means more than delivering equipment. It means adapting leak detection technology to the needs of a real production line and helping partners build stronger manufacturing capability for demanding applications.

Beta Enerji's new factory is a good example of that approach. With this delivery, Wayeal is supporting a customer that needs dependable, high-sensitivity inspection for energy and transformer-related production.
